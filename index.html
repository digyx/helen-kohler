Since starting piano at the age of 9, Helen Kohler has always shown a unique love for piano and music in general. She went to Souderton Area High School in Souderton, PA where she had the opportunities to play in chamber groups, musical pits, their Jazz Band, and accompany their choir. At age 17, she won first place in a Concerto Competition and played with the Youth Montgomery County Orchestra. She currently plays professionally with a Jazz Combo called Swing Shift. At age 18, Helen was accepted in the Baldwin Wallace Conservatory of Music for Piano Performance to study under Dr. Sunguen Kim. Prior to acceptance into Baldwin Wallace, she studied with Glenn Midgley, Nahrie Adams, and Erica Cherry.

She is also pursuing a Studio Art minor and working towards achieving fluency in French, Spanish, and Korean.
